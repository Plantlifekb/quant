Quant v1.0 — Risk Model Specification

Canonical, governed, non‑drifting

Purpose

Define the exact risk metrics, constraints, invariants, and deterministic rules used to measure and control portfolio‑level risk in Quant v1.0. This ensures stable behaviour, auditability, and alignment with the system’s non‑stochastic philosophy.

1. Risk Philosophy

Quant v1.0 uses a minimalist, deterministic risk model designed to:

avoid randomness

avoid probabilistic forecasting

avoid external risk factors

rely only on governed, internally derived metrics

maintain full transparency and reproducibility

Risk is measured, not predicted.

3. Core Risk Metrics

3.1 Volatility (Primary Risk Metric)

Definition: 20‑day population standard deviation of daily returns.

Formula: [ vol_{20d}(t) = \sqrt{\frac{1}{20} \sum_{i=1}^{20} (ret_1d(t-i) - \mu)^2} ]

Purpose:

Measures short‑term instability

Used for score normalization

3.2 Weekly Portfolio Volatility

Definition: Cross‑sectional volatility of weekly returns for the top 10.

Formula: [ port_vol_{1w}(t) = \sqrt{\frac{1}{10} \sum_{i=1}^{10} (ret_{1w}^{(i)}(t) - \mu)^2} ]

Purpose:

Measures dispersion of weekly performance

3.3 Drawdown

Definition: Peak‑to‑trough decline in cumulative return.

Formula: [ dd(t) = \frac{peak(t) - cum(t)}{peak(t)} ]

Where: [ peak(t) = \max_{0 \le s \le t} cum(s) ]

Purpose:

Measures capital impairment

4. Portfolio Constraints

4.1 Equal Weighting

All positions must be weighted: [ w_i = 0.1 ]

4.2 No Leverage

Forbidden:

leverage

margin

derivatives

4.3 No Shorting

All weights must satisfy: [ w_i \ge 0 ]

4.4 Maximum Exposure

Total exposure must equal: [ \sum_{i=1}^{10} w_i = 1 ]

5. Risk Invariants

No stochastic models

No VaR, CVaR, or probabilistic forecasts

No Monte Carlo simulations

No external factor models

No volatility targeting

No dynamic position sizing

No regime‑dependent leverage

Quant v1.0 must remain deterministic.

6. Risk Flags

6.1 High Volatility Flag

Triggered when: [ vol_{20d}(t) > 0.05 ]

6.2 High Drawdown Flag

Triggered when: [ dd(t) > 0.20 ]

6.3 Dispersion Flag

Triggered when: [ port_vol_{1w}(t) > 0.03 ]

Flags do not change portfolio construction; they are informational only.

7. Validation

7.1 Schema Validator

Ensures all risk fields exist and match expected types.

7.2 Compliance Checker

Ensures no forbidden risk models are used.

7.3 Directory Auditor

Ensures no alternative risk files exist.

8. Failure Handling

If risk metrics cannot be computed:

pipeline stops

no ranking or backtest may proceed

incident must be declared

End of Risk Model Specification
